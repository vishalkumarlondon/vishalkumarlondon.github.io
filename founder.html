Founder

I'm CEO & co-Founder of Alice Camera

As a creator, I wanted a professional camera more suited to my fast-paced content lifestyle. I wanted a camera with DSLR quality but with smartphone connectivity. One which could shoot, edit and share seamlessly. I couldn't find one. So I co-founded a computational photography startup in London to build a novel AI camera for creators like myself to streamline workflow.
