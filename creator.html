I'm a Cultural Data Scientist

I create content at the forefront of data science and AI for the Cultural and Creative Industries. I have generated over 30,000 followers on social media around my his niche as a Cultural Data Scientist. I have written over 15 blog posts and produced over 20 videos which have generated a combined 1 million views online. My flagship lecture series, Data Science for Arts and Culture, have been delivered in over 10 different locations across 5 countries.
